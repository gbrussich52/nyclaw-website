### The Real Case Study: How We Built Ainsley

**Section Header:** Case Study: 4 Days, 5 Businesses, $0 in Contractors

---

**The Problem**
Running multiple businesses — HVAC leads, SEO retainers, content automation, land clearing, apparel — means constant context-switching. Leads get missed. Content doesn't go out. Follow-ups don't happen.

Hiring people costs $5K+/month before you know if the system works. Agencies charge $3K-8K/month to manage things you could automate.

We decided to build instead.

**The Approach**
Using OpenClaw (open-source AI agent framework), Claude AI, and our own hardware (Mac Studio), we built Ainsley — a fully autonomous AI assistant that runs 24/7, manages multiple revenue streams, and remembers everything.

**Timeline:**
- Day 1: Hardware setup, OpenClaw installation, Discord integration
- Day 2: Building SOUL.md (Ainsley's identity/values), custom skill architecture
- Day 3: Deploying TerraForge lead manager + revenue tracking system
- Day 4: SEO content automation + content pipeline for Instagram/TikTok

**The Results (Verified):**

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Businesses set up with AI management | 5 |
| Days to completion | 4 |
| Money spent on contractors | $0 |
| Websites launched | 3 (nyclaw.io, TerraForge, content site) |
| Custom skills built | 5 |
| AI assistant running in production | 1 (Ainsley) |
| Revenue streams under AI management | Multiple |

**What Ainsley Does Right Now:**
- Processes TerraForge landscaping/land-clearing leads from Discord
- Tracks revenue across 5 businesses toward $100K/month goal
- Generates SEO content for client websites
- Manages Instagram/TikTok content pipeline
- Answers business questions 24/7 via Discord
- Stays quiet when there's nothing useful to say (this was harder to build than it sounds)

**The Infrastructure:**
- **Hardware:** Mac Studio (M4 Max, 36GB RAM) — runs silently in my office 24/7
- **AI:** Claude Max subscription ($100/mo)
- **Framework:** OpenClaw (open source, free)
- **Interface:** Discord (free)
- **Total extra cost beyond what we already paid:** ~$0
