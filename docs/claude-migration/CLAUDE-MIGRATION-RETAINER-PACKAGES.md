# PACKAGE A — DOCUMENTATION RETAINER
## $2,500/month

**Who this is for:** Dealerships that want to get their processes documented and systematized before automating. They're not ready for full agent management — but they know their current state is a mess and they want a foundation.

### What's Included:

**Month 1: Process Audit + SOP Library**
- We interview your top 3-5 sales reps and your sales manager to document every major workflow (lead intake, follow-up sequences, test drive process, financing conversation, delivery, post-sale follow-up)
- Output: Written SOPs for each workflow, formatted as Claude-ready skills
- Deliverable: A folder of documented processes your reps can reference — and your Claude agents can use

**Ongoing (Month 2+): Monthly SOP Optimization**
- One working session per month (60-90 min, remote) to review what's changed
- Update SOPs when inventory, pricing, or process changes
- Turn new workflows into Claude skills as they emerge
- One new Claude skill per month (from the SOP library)

**Monthly Reporting:**
- Summary of processes documented
- List of skills active in Claude
- Recommendations for what to automate next

### What This Isn't:
- We don't build or manage agents under this package
- We don't handle CRM integrations
- We don't do weekly check-ins

**Upgrade path:** Add Package B when you're ready to activate the playbook.

## PRICING RATIONALE (for internal use — don't share)

**Why $2,500/mo for docs?**
One consulting hour at any decent NYC firm is $250-500. We're doing 5-10 hours of work per month at minimum. SOPs that actually get used are worth 10x this — they reduce rep ramp time from 3 months to 3 weeks.

**Why $5,000/mo for agents?**
A single agent that saves each rep 4 hours/week = 40 hours/week saved for a 10-rep team. At $25/hr loaded cost for sales rep time, that's $1,000/week in recovered time. We're charging $5K/mo for $4K/week in value. That's a 3x+ ROI. Easy yes.

**Why $10,000/mo for full autonomy?**
One closed deal at a midsize dealership is $1,500-$3,000 in gross profit. If we help close 5-10 additional deals per month through faster follow-up, better lead coverage, and rep efficiency — the ROI is $7,500-$30,000/month. We're charging $10K. That's the frame.

Yes — 30-day notice to pause any package. No annual contract required (though we offer a 10% discount for 12-month commits).

**"What if we already have a marketing agency or AI tool?"**
Great. We work alongside your existing stack, not against it. We build on Claude specifically because it's the best reasoning model for sales contexts — your other tools don't do what we do.
