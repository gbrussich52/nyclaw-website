## SENDING GUIDE

**Best send times for dealer GMs/sales managers:**
- Tuesday–Thursday, 7:30–9:00 AM (before floor opens)
- Tuesday–Thursday, 5:30–6:30 PM (after floor closes)
- Avoid Saturdays (busiest sales day), avoid Monday mornings

**Target contacts at each dealership (in priority order):**
1. General Sales Manager (GSM) — has budget authority
2. Sales Manager — feels the daily pain most
3. GM — only if you can't reach GSM

**LinkedIn pre-warm (optional but boosts response rate):**
Connect with target 2-3 days before emailing. View their profile. No message needed — just the warm signal. Response rates jump ~15-20%.

**Personalization minimum standard:**
- Must have correct name, dealership name, location
- Must have at least 1 specific reference (brand they sell, rep count estimate, or something from their website/Google listing)
- Generic emails get deleted. Specific ones get read.
