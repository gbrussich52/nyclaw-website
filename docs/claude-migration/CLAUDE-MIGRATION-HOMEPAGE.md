## THREE VALUE PROPS (vs. staying on ChatGPT)

### Value Prop 1: Memory That Actually Works
**ChatGPT forgets your customers. Claude remembers them.**

ChatGPT's memory feature is personal — it doesn't share context across your team, doesn't retain deal history, and resets every time a new rep touches a lead. Claude Projects give your entire sales team shared context: the customer's situation, buying timeline, financing preferences, and objection history — all in one place, accessible by whoever picks up that lead next.

**Result:** Reps spend 40 minutes less per deal on context-switching. Managers stop getting "I didn't know that" excuses.

---

### Value Prop 2: Agents That Work Your Process
**ChatGPT answers questions. Claude executes workflows.**

We don't just give you better prompts. We build Claude agents that run your actual sales process: automatic email follow-ups in your voice, call log summaries pushed to your CRM, deal status updates, and trade-in evaluations — all triggered by what your reps are already doing. The agent does the admin. Your reps close deals.

**Result:** Reps go from 28% selling time to 60%+. Same headcount, more deals.

---

### Value Prop 3: Continuity When Reps Turn Over
**When a sales rep quits, ChatGPT takes their context with them. Claude doesn't.**

Dealership rep turnover is brutal — industry average is 67% annually. Every time a rep leaves, they take their ChatGPT history, their prompt tricks, and their customer context. With Claude Projects, the institutional knowledge stays. New reps ramp up in days, not weeks.

**Result:** Managers stop losing deals every time someone quits. Continuity becomes a system, not a prayer.
